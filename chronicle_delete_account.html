Delete Your Chronicle Account

Last updated: 22 June 2026

You can permanently delete your Chronicle account and all associated data using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in-app (fastest)

  1. Open Chronicle and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to Account and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm by entering your password
  4. Your account and all data are permanently deleted immediately

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address linked to your Chronicle account and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.

Email support@chronicleapp.com.au

What gets deleted

  • Your account, email, and login credentials
  • All journal and dream entries
  • All calendar events, tasks, habits, and goals
  • Mood history and birth chart / astrology data
  • Budget and expense records
  • Saved templates, birthdays, and event reminders
  • Your push notification token
What we retain (and why):

Anonymous, non-identifying technical logs (e.g. crash reports) may be retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes, but these cannot be linked back to your account once it's deleted. If you have an active subscription, Apple/Google retain billing records as required by law — this is handled entirely by their platforms, not by us.

Timeline

In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed within 30 days, and you'll receive a confirmation once your data has been removed from our systems.

Questions about this process? Contact support@chronicleapp.com.au. See our full Privacy Policy for more detail on how your data is used.
